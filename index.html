A free media server. Stream your media to devices on your home network.

  • Github

Compatible

Gerbera works with many devices within your home including your TVs, Playstations, Android & iOS phones and tablets.

Optional transcoding support converts your media on the fly to a format supported by your devices.

Powerful

Gerbera automatically extracts metadata from you music files. When combined with the power of JavaScript, you can create the layout of the collection as you want it, on all devices.

Dont want to customise? We provide a great experience by default.

MediaTomb

Gerbera is proudly build upon MediaTomb 12.1 continuing that projects great legacy.
